About Me

Hello my name is Bilgehan Bezir. I was born in Trabzon/Turkey in 1999. I am a Computer Engineer with a strong foundation in both software development and Cybersecurity, currently working as part of the Cybersecurity team at TGS (Turkish Ground Services). I hold a degree from Tekirdağ Namık Kemal University, where I graduated from the Computer Engineering Department in 2022.

My professional journey brings together two disciplines that I find deeply complementary: building robust, scalable systems and ensuring they remain secure and reliable. On the development side, which I mostly pursue as a hobby, I enjoy creating front-end interfaces using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React.js. For the back end, I design solutions using C#, ASP.NET Core MVC, Entity Framework, and MS SQL, focusing on clean architecture and maintainable systems. On the security side—my primary professional focus—I apply an engineering mindset to identify vulnerabilities, analyze system behavior, and help create more resilient digital environments.

I am by nature a continuous learner; curious, methodical, and motivated by complex problems. I maintain personal documentation of the technologies I work with and share it publicly, reflecting my belief that knowledge grows exponentially when organized and shared. My GitHub activity and numerous personal projects—a multi-panel library management system, an e-commerce platform, and many others that I keep private because they are related to my work—are expressions of this philosophy. I thrive in environments where precision matters and where I can contribute meaningfully both as an individual and as a collaborative team member. I communicate clearly, adapt quickly, and hold myself to a high standard in every project I take on.
